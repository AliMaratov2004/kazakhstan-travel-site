ANCIENT TARAZ MUSEUM

Ancient Taraz – a modern museum in the heart of a historic city

The Ancient Taraz Museum was built on the site of archaeological excavations where fragments of the old city were discovered. It combines a museum exhibition with open-air archaeological zones, where visitors can see the remains of walls, bathhouses, and other buildings from the early Middle Ages.

  • Opened: 2015
  • Exhibits: Artifacts from the 6th–12th centuries
  • Design: Modern glass building over excavations
  • Function: Museum and archaeological park

What to see?

  • 🏛 Original foundations of the ancient city
  • ⚱ A collection of ceramics, jewelry, and coins
  • 📜 Interactive panels and reconstructions

How to get there?

📍 Taraz, Tole Bi Avenue, near the Central Square
🚶 Walking distance from the city center
🚌 Public transport to “Central Park” bus stop

Visitor Information

⏰ Opening hours: 09:00–18:00, closed on Mondays
🎟️ Admission: 500 KZT (adults), 300 KZT (children)
📸 Photography is allowed

The Ancient Taraz Museum is a journey through time that lets you witness history with your own eyes.
