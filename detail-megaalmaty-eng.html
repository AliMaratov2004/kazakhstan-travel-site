MEGA ALMA-ATA & MEGA PARK

MEGA Park and MEGA Alma-Ata – the largest shopping centers in Almaty

MEGA Alma-Ata and MEGA Park are the most popular shopping and entertainment centers in Almaty. They are ideal places to spend time with family and friends, enjoy shopping, visit restaurants, or go to the cinema.

  • MEGA Alma-Ata: the largest mall with international brands 🏬
  • MEGA Park: a modern shopping center in the heart of Almaty 🌳
  • Entertainment: cinema, kids' zones, food court, ice rink

What’s Inside the Malls?

  • 🛍 Stores – Zara, H&M, Massimo Dutti, Nike, Adidas, and more
  • 🍽 Restaurants – Hardee’s, KFC, McDonald's, Italian and Asian cuisine
  • 🎬 Chaplin Cinema – IMAX and the latest movies
  • 🎠 For Kids – Happylon, ice rink, play areas
  • 🎡 Holiday events and live shows

How to Get There?

📍 MEGA Alma-Ata: Rozybakiyev Street, 247A
📍 MEGA Park: Seifullin Avenue, 483/2
🚇 Metro: “Alatau” station (for MEGA Alma-Ata)
🚕 Taxi/Bus: conveniently located in the city center

Opening Hours & Prices:

🕒 Hours: 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM
💰 Entrance: free
💰 Entertainment: from 2,000 KZT

MEGA Alma-Ata and MEGA Park are the top destinations in Almaty for shopping, leisure, and family fun! 🛍🎡🎬
