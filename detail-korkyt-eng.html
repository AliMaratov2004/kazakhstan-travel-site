KORKYT ATA

Korkyt Ata Memorial Complex

This spiritual and cultural complex is dedicated to the great Kazakh philosopher, thinker, and poet Korkyt Ata. The complex is notable for its unique architecture and acoustic effect.

  • Location: near Korkyt railway station, Torekty
  • Opened: 1980
  • Feature: The sound of the kuy (melody) echoes around the complex
