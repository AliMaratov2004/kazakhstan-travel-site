CENTRAL SQUARE

The heart of Kyzylorda – a place for gatherings, celebrations, and public events

Central Square in Kyzylorda is a lively public space where the city's main events, parades, concerts, and fairs are held. Designed with a modern style, it is a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike for walking and leisure.

  • Function: Venue for public events and leisure
  • Features: Fountains, monuments, flagpole
  • Location: In the center of the administrative district

What to see?

  • 🎆 Festive concerts and national celebrations
  • 🌳 Green alleys and art installations nearby
  • 📸 Panoramic photo opportunities with urban architecture

How to get there?

📍 Kyzylorda, N. Nazarbayev Avenue
🚕 Accessible via taxi or local bus routes

Visitor Information

⏰ Open 24/7
🎟️ Free access for all visitors

Central Square is a symbol of modernity and culture in Kyzylorda, where the past and future of the region meet.
