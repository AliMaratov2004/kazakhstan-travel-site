LIBRARY OF THE FIRST PRESIDENT

Library of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan

The Library of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan is a modern scientific and cultural center that preserves unique documents, books, and archival materials related to the history of independent Kazakhstan. It is not just a library, but a multifunctional complex that includes a museum, exhibition halls, a research center, and educational spaces.

  • Opened: 2014
  • Total Area: 30,000 m²
  • Architect: Norman Foster
  • Materials: Glass, metal, concrete
  • Style: Futurism with national ornament elements

What to see?

✅ Book Collection – rare editions, scientific works, and personal books of Nursultan Nazarbayev 📚
✅ Independence Museum – exhibits about the formation of Kazakhstan as an independent state 🇰🇿
✅ Multimedia Halls – modern technologies, virtual tours, and interactive panels 🖥
✅ Scientific Research Hall – a center for students and researchers working with archives 🔬

Location:

📍 Address: 1, Älikhan Bökeykhan Street, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: № 2, 7, 12, 27, 32, taxi

Entrance Fee:

💰 Free (for all visitors)
