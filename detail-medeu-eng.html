Medeu – The Highest Ice Rink in the World

Medeu is a legendary high-mountain ice rink located in the gorge of the Ile Alatau. It is one of the most famous places in Almaty, attracting tourists and athletes all year round.

  • Altitude: 1,691 m above sea level
  • Ice area: 10,500 m²
  • Capacity: 8,500 spectators
  • Opened: 1972

What to Do?

  • ❄ Winter: ice skating, hockey, public skating ⛸🏒
  • 🌿 Summer: hiking in the gorge, climbing the "Health Stairs" (842 steps) 🚶‍♂🏔

How to Get There?

📍 Address: Medeu Gorge, Almaty
🚌 Bus No. 12 from Republic Square
🚖 Taxi (15–20 minutes from the city center)

Prices

💰 Ice rink (with skate rental): 2,500 ₸ (adults), 1,500 ₸ (children)
💰 Health Stairs: free

Medeu is a unique place for rest and sports where you can breathe fresh mountain air and enjoy stunning views! ⛸🏔✨
