ANNUNCIATION CHURCH

Orthodox Sanctuary of Aktau – Annunciation Church

The Annunciation Church is the main Orthodox church in the Mangystau Region and an important spiritual site and architectural landmark in the city of Aktau.

  • Consecration: 1995
  • Architecture: Traditional Russian style with domes and crosses
  • Function: Active parish of the Russian Orthodox Church
  • Features: Bell tower, iconostasis, and church shop

What to See?

  • 🕍 Beautiful frescoes and icons inside the church
  • 🔔 Bell tower and evening services
  • 🧘‍♂️ Peaceful atmosphere and well-kept grounds

How to Get There?

📍 Aktau, 4th microdistrict
🚶 10-minute walk from the promenade
🚌 Public transport available from the city center

Visitor Information

⏰ Open daily from 08:00 to 19:00
🎟️ Free entry
🧥 Modest clothing is recommended

The Annunciation Church is not just a place of worship, but also a cultural landmark for all guests of Aktau.
