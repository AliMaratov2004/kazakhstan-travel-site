BATYRKHAN SHUKENOV MONUMENT

Monument to the Legendary Kazakh Singer and Composer

The Batyrkhan Shukenov Monument is a touching tribute to one of Kazakhstan's brightest musical icons. Located in his hometown of Kyzylorda, the monument has become a favorite spot for fans and tourists alike.

  • Year Unveiled: 2018
  • Material: Bronze
  • Sculptor: Renowned Kazakh artists

Interesting Facts

  • 🎷 The statue features Batyrkhan with a saxophone — his favorite instrument
  • 🎵 His music plays around the monument area
  • 📍 A popular photo spot and meeting point

How to Get There?

📍 Kyzylorda, Batyrkhan Shukenov Street
🚕 Easily accessible by taxi or public transport from the city center

Visitor Info

⏰ Open 24/7
💵 Free for all visitors
📸 Recommended for family walks and tourists

The Batyrkhan Monument is more than just a statue — it is a heartfelt symbol of the people's love for an outstanding artist.
