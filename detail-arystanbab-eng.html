ARYSTAN BAB

Arystan Bab Mausoleum — spiritual legacy of the Turkic world

Arystan Bab is one of the most revered Sufi saints of Kazakhstan and the mentor of Khoja Ahmed Yassawi. His mausoleum is a pilgrimage site and a nationally significant spiritual monument.

  • Constructed: 12th century, restored in the 19th–20th centuries
  • Architecture: domed, with Eastern architectural elements
  • Location: Shaulder village, Turkistan Region
  • Significance: Pilgrimage site, spiritual center

What to see?

  • 🕌 Main dome and the saint's tomb
  • 📜 Historical inscriptions and plaques
  • 🌿 Quiet courtyards for prayer

How to get there?

📍 Turkistan Region, near Otyrar
🚗 Accessible by car or tourist bus

Visitor Info

⏰ Entry: Free
🧕 Dress modestly, especially for women

Arystan Bab is a sacred place of faith and historical memory, a must-visit site during your journey to Turkistan.
