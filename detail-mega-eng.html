MEGA Silk Way

The Largest Shopping and Entertainment Center in Astana

MEGA Silk Way is the largest shopping and entertainment center in Astana, combining shopping, fun, and leisure for the whole family. Located near the EXPO city, it has become one of the most popular leisure spots in the capital.

  • Opened: 2017
  • Area: over 140,000 m²
  • Number of stores: more than 200
  • Features: winter garden, ice rink, petting zoo

What to See and Do?

✅ Boutiques and branded stores 👗
✅ Food court with diverse cuisines 🍔
✅ Entertainment zones for children 🎠
✅ Kinopark cinema 🎬
✅ Happy Land family park 🎡

Location:

📍 Address: Mangilik El Avenue, 62, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: No. 12, 51, 53, 54

Entrance Fee:

💰 Free (entertainment services are charged separately)
