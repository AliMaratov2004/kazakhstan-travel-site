ABAY MONUMENT

Monument to Abay Kunanbayev – Great Kazakh Poet and Thinker

The Abay monument in Semey is a symbol of Kazakhstan's spiritual and cultural heritage. Erected in honor of the great poet, philosopher, and enlightener Abay Kunanbayev, the statue is located in the city center, near his literary-memorial museum.

  • Year of installation: 1958
  • Material: Bronze and granite
  • Height: Over 10 meters
  • Author: Sculptor H. Nauryzbayev

What to see nearby?

  • 📚 Abay Literary-Memorial Museum
  • 🌳 A landscaped alley with benches and lanterns
  • 📸 Great spots for photography and walking

How to get there?

📍 Semey, Abay Avenue, near the theater and museum
🚌 Public transport: buses to the city center

Useful information

⏰ Open 24/7
🎟️ Free entry
🌞 Best visited during the daytime

The Abay Monument is more than a sculpture – it’s a place that honors national culture and the memory of a great son of the Kazakh people.
