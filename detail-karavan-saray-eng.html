CARAVANSERAI

Historic Torqut Caravanserai – Gateway of the Silk Road

The Torqut Caravanserai was one of the key stops for trade caravans crossing southern Kazakhstan. Located in a desert region, it offered shelter, food, and water for merchants and travelers along the Great Silk Road.

  • Period: 10th–12th centuries
  • Architecture: adobe walls, inner courtyard, towers
  • Purpose: rest stop for caravans, protection, and trade exchange

What to See?

  • 🏰 Reconstructed sections of the ancient complex
  • 📜 Archaeological artifacts and interactive exhibits
  • 🌅 Views of the desert landscape

How to Get There?

📍 Turkistan Region, near the village of Torqut
🚗 By car: around 1 hour from Turkistan by highway
🚌 Tourist tours available from Shymkent and Turkistan

Facilities

⛺ Rest areas available on-site
🎟️ Entry: 1,000 KZT (adults), 500 KZT (children)
🕰️ Opening hours: 09:00 – 18:00 daily

The Torqut Caravanserai is a unique destination where the spirit of the Silk Road comes to life.
