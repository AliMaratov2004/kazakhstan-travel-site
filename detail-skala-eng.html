ROCKY TRAIL

A scenic nature path perfect for active travelers

The Rocky Trail in Mangystau is a unique natural route passing through stunning rocky mountains and canyons. It is a great spot for hiking, photo tours, and exploring the region's geological features.

  • Length: about 5 km
  • Difficulty: moderate
  • Highlights: cliffs, mountains, and rare plants
  • Popularity: well-known among tourists and locals

Things to do

  • 🥾 Hiking and trekking
  • 📷 Photo shoots among rocky landscapes
  • 🌄 Catching sunrise or sunset views from above

How to get there?

📍 Mangystau region, not far from Aktau
🚗 Accessible by off-road vehicles
🧭 A guide is recommended for safety

Useful information

⛺ Suitable for day trips
☀ Best seasons: spring and autumn
🥤 Bring water and comfortable shoes

The Rocky Trail is the perfect destination for adventurous travelers seeking to discover the natural wonders of Mangystau.
