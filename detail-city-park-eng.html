CENTRAL CITY PARK

Central Park — the green heart of Semey

The Central City Park in Semey is one of the most beloved places for walks, relaxation, and family leisure. The spacious area with trees, fountains, attractions, and alleys creates a cozy and calm atmosphere.

  • Area: over 15 hectares
  • Features: fountains, stage, bike rental
  • Attractions: children's swings, carousels, mini-zoo
  • Food: cafes, kiosks, ice cream

What to do?

  • 🌳 Stroll through the alleys
  • 🚴‍♀️ Ride bikes and scooters
  • 🎡 Visit attractions
  • 📸 Take photos in picturesque corners

How to get there?

📍 City center of Semey, Abay Street
🚍 Accessible by public transport or on foot

Entry & Hours

🕒 Open daily from 08:00 to 22:00
💰 Free entry (attractions are paid separately)

The park is perfect for family outings, outdoor walks, and cultural events!
