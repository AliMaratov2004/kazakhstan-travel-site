ARAI CITY MALL

The modern shopping and entertainment center of Kyzylorda

Arai City Mall is the largest shopping and entertainment center in Kyzylorda, combining numerous shops, cafes, entertainment zones, and a cinema. It has become a favorite destination for family recreation and shopping.

  • Stores: clothing, shoes, electronics, cosmetics
  • Entertainment: children's play area, bowling, cinema
  • Cafes and Restaurants: fast food, coffee shops, world cuisines
  • Parking: free, multi-level

Why visit?

  • 🛍️ Great shopping experience
  • 🎬 Family-friendly cinema screenings
  • 🍔 Diverse food options
  • 🎡 Safe and fun children's area

How to get there?

📍 Kyzylorda, N. Nazarbayev Avenue, 18
🚌 Public transport: routes #2, #12, #19
🚕 Taxis available via Yandex Go and InDrive

Working Hours

⏰ Daily from 10:00 to 22:00
🎟️ Entry: Free

Arai City Mall is the heart of modern Kyzylorda, offering something for everyone.
