Foreign embassies in Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan has established diplomatic relations with many countries of the world, and dozens of embassies and consulates of foreign states are located on the territory of the country. Embassies support their citizens and deal with visa issues, diplomatic missions and the development of international cooperation.

In this section you will find information about the addresses, contact details and services of all embassies in Kazakhstan. Visa issue for you, whether you need consular assistance or communication with representatives of your country – all the important data is collected here.
