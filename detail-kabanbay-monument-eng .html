KABANBAY BATYR MONUMENT

Monument to the Great Commander and Hero of the Kazakh Steppe

The Kabanbay Batyr monument in Semey is a symbol of bravery, patriotism, and heroism of the Kazakh people. Erected in honor of one of the most respected military leaders of the 18th century, the monument serves as a site of reverence and historical education.

  • Inauguration: 2010
  • Material: Bronze and granite
  • Height: About 10 meters
  • Location: Central Semey
