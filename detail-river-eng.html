ASTANA EMBANKMENT

Astana Embankment – the heart of the capital along the Yesil River

The Astana embankment is one of the most beautiful and popular places in the city. Here you can take a walk, enjoy panoramic views of the capital, and feel the atmosphere of a modern metropolis. It is a perfect spot for both leisurely strolls and active recreation.

  • Location: Along the Yesil River
  • Length: About 6 km
  • Main attractions: Baiterek, bridges over Yesil, “Stolichny” park

What to see and do?

✅ Scenic views of the Yesil River – especially beautiful at night with illumination 🌆
✅ Pedestrian and bike paths – great for walking and sports 🚶‍♂🚴
✅ Boat rides and water bikes – a chance to see the city from the water 🚤
✅ Street performers and musicians – create a festive atmosphere 🎶
✅ Astana City Park 📸
✅ Atyrau Bridge

Address:

📍 Starts in the city center near Independence Square and stretches along the Yesil River
🚖 Transport: buses (No. 2, 10, 25, 20, 32, 37), taxi

Entrance fee:

💰 Free (entertainment and rental services are paid separately)
