MiG-21 MONUMENT

A Monument to the Legendary Soviet-Era Fighter Jet

The "MiG-21" monument is one of Aktau's iconic landmarks, erected in honor of aviation and its contribution to the region's development. The aircraft is mounted on a pedestal, symbolizing power, speed, and technological advancement.

  • Type: Military fighter jet
  • Production: USSR, 1960s
  • Model: MiG-21 — supersonic interceptor
  • Location: Aktau City Park

Why It's Special

  • ✈️ Real aircraft displayed on a pedestal
  • 📸 Popular spot for photos with children
  • 🕊️ Symbol of memory and patriotism

How to Get There

📍 Aktau, near Respublika Avenue
🚶 10 minutes walk or by car from the city center

Useful Information

🔍 Can be viewed from all sides, but climbing is prohibited
💡 Lit at night
📷 Looks great during sunset

The MiG-21 Monument is a proud tribute to Soviet aviation and a key symbol in Aktau's urban landscape.
