ZHUMBAKTAS

Zhumbaktas Rock – the Mystery of Burabay

Zhumbaktas (translated from Kazakh as “Mysterious Stone”) is an unusual rock formation standing in the middle of Lake Burabay. Its shape is astonishing: from different angles, it resembles either a young girl or an old man, inspiring many legends and interpretations.

  • Height: around 18 meters
  • Material: granite
  • Age: millions of years
  • Shape: changes depending on the viewing angle

What to See?

  • 📸 Unique shape – different images appear from water and land
  • 🌅 Lake Burabay panorama – perfect photos at sunset
  • 🛶 Boat ride – best angle for photography and video

How to Get There?

📍 Location: middle of Lake Burabay, Akmola Region
🚗 By car: about 3 hours from Astana to Burabay
🚶 On foot or by boat: 15–30 minutes from the shore

Hours and Prices

🕒 Access: open 24/7
💰 Entry: free, boat rides from 2,000 KZT

Zhumbaktas is one of the most mysterious and photogenic spots in Burabay where nature seems to speak in riddles! 🪨✨
