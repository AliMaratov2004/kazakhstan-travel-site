Khan Shatyr

Khan Shatyr – the largest shopping and entertainment center in Astana

Khan Shatyr is the world’s largest tent-like structure and one of the iconic landmarks of Astana. It is not just a shopping center – it features parks, attractions, and even a tropical beach!

  • Opened: 2010
  • Height: 150 meters
  • Total Area: 127,000 m²
  • Architect: Norman Foster
  • Materials: Transparent ETFE membrane

What to see and do?

✅ Boutiques and stores – over 200 international and Kazakhstani brands 🛍
✅ Hypermarket – a large supermarket for groceries and household items 🛒
✅ Chaplin Cinemas – modern halls and comfortable seating 🎬
✅ "Sky Beach Club" – a tropical beach with white sand imported from the Maldives! 🏖
✅ Attractions and kids' areas – including a Ferris wheel and play zones 🎡
✅ Restaurants and cafes – cuisines from around the world 🍽

Location:

📍 Address: 37 Turan Avenue, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: № 12, 21, 32, 53, 54, taxi

Entrance Fee:

💰 Free (entertainment and rides are charged separately)
