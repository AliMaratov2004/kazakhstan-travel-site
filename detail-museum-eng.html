NATIONAL MUSEUM

National Museum of the Republic of Kazakhstan

Located in Astana, the National Museum of the Republic of Kazakhstan is the largest and most modern museum in Central Asia. It was established to preserve and promote the historical and cultural heritage of Kazakhstan.

  • Established: 2014
  • Total area: over 74,000 m²
  • Number of halls: 11 thematic halls
  • Architect: B.I. Zhumatayev

What to see?

✅ Hall of Kazakhstan History – artifacts from ancient times to the present
✅ Hall of Gold and Jewelry – Scythian gold and treasures
✅ Ethnography Hall – lifestyle and traditions of the Kazakh people
✅ Contemporary Art Hall – works by Kazakhstani artists and sculptors
✅ Children's area, quest rooms, and digital installations for kids

Location:

📍 Address: Tauelsizdik Ave, 54, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: No. 18, 21, 35, 47, taxi

Entrance Fee:

💰 Adults: 1000 KZT
💰 Children: 500 KZT
💰 Discounts: Free (children under 5, pensioners, students)
