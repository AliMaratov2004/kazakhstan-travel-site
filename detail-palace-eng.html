Palace of Peace and Reconciliation

Palace of Peace and Reconciliation – Symbol of Unity in Kazakhstan

The Palace of Peace and Reconciliation is a unique pyramid-shaped building in Astana, symbolizing tolerance, unity, and interethnic harmony. It hosts global and traditional religions' leaders congresses, international conferences, exhibitions, concerts, and cultural festivals.

  • Opened: 2006
  • Height: 62 meters
  • Area: 28,000 m²
  • Architect: Norman Foster
  • Materials: glass, metal, concrete

What to see?

✅ Golden Hall Atrium – a spacious central hall with elegant interior ✨
✅ Hazret Sultan Opera Hall – a multifunctional concert and conference hall with 1,500 seats 🎭
✅ Assembly Hall of the People of Kazakhstan – venue for international meetings and forums 🤝
✅ Hall of Religious Traditions – space showcasing symbols of world religions ☪✝✡
✅ Peace Dome Observation Deck – panoramic view of Astana with stained-glass doves 🕊

Location:

📍 Address: Tauelsizdik Avenue, 57, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: No. 29, 19, 40, 4, 4a, 29a, 3, 50, 32, 58, taxis available
