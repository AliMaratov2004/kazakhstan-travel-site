KOK TOBE

Kok Tobe – Almaty's Panoramic Hill

Kok Tobe is a famous hill in Almaty that offers the best view of the city and the Zailiyskiy Alatau mountains. It features an amusement park, restaurants, and Almaty’s iconic monument to The Beatles.

  • Elevation: 1,100 m above sea level
  • Main attraction: Kok Tobe TV Tower (372 m)
  • Park opened: 2006

What to See?

  • 🌆 Observation deck – panoramic view of Almaty
  • 🎡 Attractions – Ferris wheel, rope park
  • 🎸 The Beatles monument – a favorite tourist spot
  • 🛍 Souvenir shops – national gifts and crafts

How to Get There?

📍 Location: southeast Almaty
🚡 Cable car: from the Palace of the Republic (5 minutes, 1,500 ₸)
🚗 By car: access via the eastern road

Prices

💰 Park entrance: free
💰 Cable car: 1,500 ₸ (adult), 1,000 ₸ (child)
💰 Attractions: from 1,000 ₸

Kok Tobe is a perfect place to enjoy nature, city views, and fun activities! 🌆🌿🎡
