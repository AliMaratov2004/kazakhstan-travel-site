ABAI MUSEUM

State Historical-Cultural and Literary Memorial Museum-Reserve of Abai

This museum serves as a center for the study of the life and work of the great Kazakh poet and educator Abai Kunanbayev. Located in the city of Semey, the museum-reserve includes not only exhibition halls but also historic buildings related to Abai and his circle.

  • Opened: 1940
  • Collection: Over 20,000 exhibits
  • Sections: Abai's life and work, history of the Semipalatinsk region

What to see?

  • 📖 Personal belongings and manuscripts of Abai
  • 🎨 Paintings, books, historical documents
  • 🏛 Archival materials about Abai's family and companions

How to get there?

📍 Semey, Auezov Street, 112
🚕 By taxi or public transport from the city center

Visitor Information

⏰ Opening hours: 09:00 - 18:00, closed on Mondays
🎟️ Ticket price: Adults – 700 KZT, Students – 400 KZT
📸 Photography is allowed

The Abai Museum is a unique place to connect with the cultural heritage of Kazakhstan and the legacy of a great poet.
