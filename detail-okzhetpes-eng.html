OKZHETPES

Okzhetpes Rock – Symbol of Burabay and Steppe Legend

Okzhetpes is a unique rock formation towering over Lake Burabay. Its name translates to “the arrow won't reach,” originating from a legend about a brave girl who chose freedom over captivity.

  • Height: about 300 meters
  • Location: on the shore of Lake Burabay
  • Material: granite weathered by time

What to See?

  • ⛰ Rock shape – resembles a sleeping giant or warrior’s profile
  • 📸 Viewpoints – panoramic views of Burabay and the forests
  • 🗿 Legendary outlines – different images visible in the rock

What to Do?

  • 🚶‍♂️ Walking and photoshoots
  • 🧗‍♂️ Rock climbing
  • 🛶 Boating

How to Get There?

📍 Burabay National Park
🚗 By car: 3 hours from Astana
🚶 On foot: 15–20 minutes from the center of Burabay

Hours and Prices:

🕒 Access: year-round
💰 Entry: free of charge

Okzhetpes is a natural wonder and one of Burabay’s must-see landmarks! ⛰✨
