CARAVEL MONUMENT

Symbol of Romance and Exploration – Caravel in Aktau

The "Caravel" monument is a unique sculpture shaped like a sailing ship, located on the Aktau promenade. It symbolizes the pursuit of discovery, freedom, and the romance of maritime adventures.

  • Type: Decorative monument
  • Location: Aktau waterfront
  • Popularity: One of the most photographed places
  • Purpose: Art object and city symbol

What makes it special?

  • 🚢 Realistic sailing ship shape
  • 🌅 Stunning sunsets with sea backdrop
  • 📸 Perfect spot for photoshoots

How to get there?

📍 Aktau promenade, near the beach
🚶 5–10 minutes walk from the central street
🚕 Accessible by taxi or car

Useful information

💡 Illuminated at night
🧒 Suitable for family walks
🚫 Climbing the monument is prohibited

The Caravel Monument is a symbol of the endless sea and the spirit of freedom, inspiring both locals and visitors of Aktau.
