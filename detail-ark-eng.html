MANGILIK EL

Mangilik El Triumph Arch – Symbol of Kazakhstan's Independence

The Mangilik El Triumph Arch is one of the most iconic architectural landmarks in Astana, symbolizing the nation’s independence and unity. The arch represents the country’s progress, intergenerational continuity, and aspiration for a bright future.

  • Opened: 2011
  • Height: 20 meters
  • Total Area: 1,961 m²
  • Architectural Style: Neoclassicism with Kazakh ornamental elements
  • Materials: White marble

What to see?

✅ "Qazaq Eli" and "Zhasampazdyk" bas-reliefs – depicting Kazakhstan’s history and development 🏛
✅ Statues of warrior-heroes – representing the country’s heroic past ⚔
✅ Memory Hall – an exhibition dedicated to the history of independence 📜
✅ Observation deck – panoramic view of Astana 🌆

Location:

📍 Address: Mangilik El Avenue, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: № 12, 21, 32, 51, or taxi

Entrance Fee:

💰 Adults: 500 ₸
💰 Children: 300 ₸
💰 Free entry: children under 6, pensioners, and persons with disabilities
