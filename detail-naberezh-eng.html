KYZYLORDA EMBANKMENT

A modern space for walks and leisure by the Syr Darya River

The Kyzylorda Embankment is one of the city's most beloved places by both locals and visitors. The long pedestrian zone, scenic bridges, benches, bike paths, and lighting create a cozy and comfortable atmosphere.

  • Length: over 2 km along the Syr Darya River
  • Infrastructure: well-lit paths, benches, and parks
  • Activities: walking, cycling, and scooter riding
  • Location: city center

What to see?

  • 🌉 Modern bridges and architectural features
  • 🌳 Green zones and shaded relaxation areas
  • 🚶 A perfect place for family and evening strolls

How to get there?

📍 Kyzylorda city center, along the Syr Darya embankment
🚖 Accessible by taxi and public transport

Fun fact

🌊 The embankment is a popular spot for photoshoots, meetups, and city celebrations.

The Kyzylorda Embankment is the perfect place to relax and enjoy nature in the heart of the city.
