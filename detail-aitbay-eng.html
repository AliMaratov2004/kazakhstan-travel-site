AITBAY MOSQUE

Aitbay Mosque — Architectural Heritage of Kyzylorda

Aitbay Mosque is one of the oldest mosques in Kyzylorda, built in the 19th century in traditional Eastern style. Its unique architecture and cultural significance make it an important symbol of the region's history and spirituality.

  • Year Built: 1878
  • Architecture: Eastern style, brickwork
  • Features: Unique dome and carved ornaments
  • Status: Functioning mosque and cultural heritage site

What to See?

  • 🕌 Historical interior of the mosque
  • 🧱 Original brickwork and carvings
  • 📸 Photo zones for tourists

How to Get There?

📍 Address: Kyzylorda, Aitbay Street
🚶 10-minute walk from the city center
🚕 Taxi and bus routes available

Useful Information

⏰ Opening Hours: Daily, 09:00 AM to 08:00 PM
🎟️ Entry: Free
🧕 Modest dress is recommended when entering

Aitbay Mosque is not just a religious site, but a symbol of Kyzylorda's spiritual and architectural heritage.
