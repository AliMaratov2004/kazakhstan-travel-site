T. RYSKULOV PARK

Central city park for relaxation in Taraz

Named after Turar Ryskulov, this park is one of the most beloved places for both locals and visitors of Taraz. It offers a mix of greenery, entertainment zones, and cultural events, making it a hub for leisure and recreation.

  • Location: Central part of the city
  • Features: Alleys, fountains, stage, amusement rides
  • Popularity: A favorite spot for family outings

What can you find?

  • 🌳 Walking paths and rest areas
  • 🎠 Attractions for children and adults
  • 🎤 Summer stage for cultural events
  • 🍦 Cafés, street food, and souvenir stalls

How to get there?

📍 Taraz city, Baizak Batyr Street
🚌 By public transport, taxi, or on foot

Visitor Information

⏰ Opening hours: Daily from 08:00 to 22:00
💰 Admission: Free, attractions paid separately

T. Ryskulov Park is the green heart of Taraz where you can relax, have fun, and feel the vibrant spirit of the city.
