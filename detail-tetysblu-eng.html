TETYSBLU THEME PARK

Modern water park and theme park on the Aktau coast

Tetysblu is one of the largest theme parks in Central Asia, located on the shores of the Caspian Sea. The complex includes a water park, family rides, restaurants, and relaxation zones.

  • Type: Theme park and water park
  • Opened: 2021
  • Area: over 20 hectares
  • Location: Aktau Resort tourist zone

What does the park offer?

  • 🎢 Roller coasters, carousels, and children's zones
  • 💦 Giant slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers
  • 🍽️ Restaurants, cafes, and relaxation areas
  • 🎉 Concerts, shows, and evening performances

How to get there?

📍 Aktau, tourist zone near Rixos Hotel
🚗 Taxi from city center – about 10–15 minutes

Prices and Hours

🕘 Open daily from 10:00 to 22:00
💳 Entry: from 5,000 KZT (depending on zones)

Tetysblu is a place where everyone finds joy. Perfect for family and youth seaside vacations!
