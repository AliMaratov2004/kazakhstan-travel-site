SHYMKENT ZOO

Shymkent Zoo – One of the Largest Zoos in Kazakhstan

Shymkent Zoo spans 34 hectares and is home to more than 150 animal species. It's one of the best family destinations in Southern Kazakhstan for learning about wildlife and spending time in nature.

  • Area: 34 hectares
  • Animal species: 150+
  • Features: Safari zone, exotic animals
  • Opened: 1980

What to See?

  • 🦁 Lions, tigers, bears
  • 🦓 Antelopes and giraffes
  • 🐍 Reptile terrarium
  • 🦜 Birds and monkeys

How to Get There?

📍 Shymkent city, Tole Bi Avenue, 173
🚌 Public transport, taxi, or private car

Opening Hours and Prices

⏰ Daily from 09:00 to 19:00
💰 Admission: Adults – 800 KZT, Children – 400 KZT

Shymkent Zoo is a great opportunity to see animals from around the world and enjoy family time in nature! 🐾
