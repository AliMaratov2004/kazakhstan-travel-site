SHYMBULAK

Shymbulak – Kazakhstan's premier ski resort

Shymbulak is the largest ski resort in Kazakhstan, located in the scenic gorge of the Ile-Alatau mountains. It is popular among skiers, snowboarders, and mountain nature lovers.

  • Elevation: 2,260–3,200 m
  • Tracks: 12 km, varying levels of difficulty
  • Lifts: 7 (gondolas and chairlifts)
  • Temperature: winter –5°C, summer +20°C

Activities

  • ❄ Winter: skiing, snowboarding, freeride, tubing 🎿🏂
  • 🌿 Summer: hiking, cycling, paragliding 🚶‍♂🚴

How to get there?

📍 Address: Medeu Gorge, Almaty
🚠 Lift: from "Medeu" Ice Rink (4.5 km)
🚌 Bus No. 12 from Republic Square

Prices

💰 Gondola lift: 5,000 KZT (adult), 3,000 KZT (child)
💰 Day ski-pass: from 10,000 KZT

Shymbulak is the perfect destination for active recreation year-round! 🏔❄🌞
