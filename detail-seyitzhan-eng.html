SEYITZHAN QARI MOSQUE

A grand mosque in the heart of Shymkent

The Seyitzhan Qari Yeszhanuly Mosque is one of the newest and most beautiful mosques in southern Kazakhstan. It was built in honor of a prominent religious figure and is now recognized as a symbol of the city’s spiritual revival.

  • Opened: 2023
  • Capacity: over 3,000 people
  • Architecture: blend of classical Islamic style and modern elements
  • Features: prayer halls, madrasa, lecture rooms

What to See?

  • 🕌 Luxurious interior with stained glass and carpets
  • 🌙 Cozy courtyard for relaxation and conversation
  • 📚 Educational programs, lectures, and courses

Where is it Located?

📍 Shymkent, Bäyditbek Bi Street
🚗 Easy access with large parking area

This is not just a religious center, but a place of spiritual inspiration and peace. 🕋✨
