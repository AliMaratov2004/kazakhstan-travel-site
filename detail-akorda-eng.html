AKORDA

Akorda Residence – a symbol of Kazakhstan’s power and dignity

Akorda is the official residence of the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan and one of the most important architectural landmarks in the country. Located in the heart of Astana, it symbolizes statehood, stability, and the development of Kazakhstan.

  • Year of construction: 2004
  • Height: 80 meters (including the spire)
  • Total area: 36,720 m²
  • Architectural style: Classical style with elements of national ornament
  • Materials: Marble, granite, glass

What’s inside?

The residence is not open to the public, but inside it includes:
✅ Oval Hall – venue for official meetings and negotiations
✅ International Summit Hall – hosts major diplomatic events
✅ Delegation Reception Hall – for official visits by foreign leaders
✅ Presidential Office – workplace of the Head of State
✅ Press Center – for briefings and national addresses

What can be seen from the outside?

✅ Square in front of the residence – spacious area with fountains and greenery 🌳
✅ Panoramic view of Baiterek – a great photo spot 📸
✅ Yesil River – a picturesque embankment nearby 🌊

Location:

📍 Address: 6, Mangilik El Avenue, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: № 10, 12, 21, 35

Can you visit?

💰 The residence is not open to tourists, but you can admire it from outside and take beautiful photos 📷
