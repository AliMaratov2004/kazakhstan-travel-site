AILAND WATERPARK

Ailand Waterpark – Water Fun in the Heart of Astana

Ailand is the largest indoor waterpark in Kazakhstan, operating all year round. It is part of the "Duman" entertainment complex, which also includes an oceanarium, 5D cinema, and various attractions. You can enjoy pools, extreme slides, and a relaxing area at any time of the year.

  • Opened: 2003
  • Total area: over 6,000 m²
  • Water temperature: +28–30°C year-round
  • Pool depth: from 60 cm to 1.8 m

What can you find at Ailand?

✅ Wave pool – simulates real ocean waves
✅ Extreme slides – 6 water attractions of varying heights and speeds
✅ Lazy river – gentle flow for relaxation
✅ Kids' zone – safe slides and pools for toddlers
✅ Jacuzzi & SPA zone – for complete relaxation
✅ Food court – refreshments and snacks available

Location:

📍 Address: 4 Kabanbai Batyr Avenue, Astana, Kazakhstan
🚖 Buses: No. 2, 21, 32, 40, taxi

Entrance Fee:

Weekdays:
💰 Adults – 6,000 KZT
💰 Children – 4,000 KZT
Weekends & Holidays:
💰 Adults – 7,000 KZT
💰 Children – 5,000 KZT

Ailand is the perfect spot for family leisure, where you can swim, slide, and enjoy a great time any season of the year! 🌊🏖✨
