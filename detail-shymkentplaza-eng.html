SHYMKENT PLAZA

Shymkent Plaza — the heart of city life

Shymkent Plaza is a modern shopping and entertainment center located in the very heart of Shymkent. It features a variety of shops, cafes, a cinema, and relaxation zones.

  • Floors: 4
  • Area: 35,000 m²
  • Brands: Zara, H&M, LC Waikiki, and more
  • Entertainment: cinema, food court, kids' zone

Things to Do

  • 🛍️ Shopping in boutiques
  • ☕ Enjoy coffee with a city view
  • 🎬 Watch movies at the cinema
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family fun activities

How to Get There?

📍 Kazhybek Bi Street, Shymkent
🚌 Accessible by bus or taxi from anywhere in the city

Opening Hours:

⏰ Daily from 10:00 to 22:00

Shymkent Plaza is the perfect place for shopping, socializing, and relaxing.
