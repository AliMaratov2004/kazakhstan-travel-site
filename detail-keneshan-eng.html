KENESKAN QAZHY MOSQUE

A unique mosque shaped like an open Quran

The Keneskan Qazhy Mosque in Taraz is a striking piece of architecture designed in the form of an open Quran. Its modern design and spiritual symbolism immediately draw attention.

  • Shape: Open book (Quran)
  • Materials: Glass, concrete, and marble
  • Function: Active mosque and architectural landmark

What to see?

  • 🕌 Unique exterior design in the shape of a Quran
  • 🧱 Spacious prayer hall
  • 🌙 Modern interiors with Islamic patterns

How to get there?

📍 Taraz, Tashkent Street
🚕 Accessible by taxi or bus from the city center

Visitor Information

⏰ Open daily from 08:00 to 21:00
🎟️ Free entry
📸 Photography is allowed

The Keneskan Qazhy Mosque is a modern symbol of spirituality in Taraz and a must-see architectural attraction.
