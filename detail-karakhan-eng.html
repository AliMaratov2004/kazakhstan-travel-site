KARAKHAN MAUSOLEUM

A prominent architectural monument from the 10th–11th centuries

The Karakhan Mausoleum is located in the center of Taraz and is considered one of the city's main landmarks. Built in honor of a representative of the Karakhanid dynasty, it is a prime example of medieval Islamic architecture featuring carved bricks and arched elements.

  • Construction period: 10th–11th centuries
  • Style: Central Asian Islamic architecture
  • Materials: Fired brick
  • Function: Tomb of a ruler, cultural heritage site

What to see?

  • 🧱 Unique decorative brick patterns
  • 📜 Historical plaque and the legend of Karakhan
  • 🌿 Cozy park surrounding the mausoleum

How to get there?

📍 Address: Tole Bi Street, Taraz
🚕 Taxi or public transport from any part of the city

Visitor Information

⏰ Opening hours: Open 24/7
🎟️ Admission: Free

The Karakhan Mausoleum is a place where history and spirituality meet — a must-visit for anyone interested in the legacy of ancient Taraz.
