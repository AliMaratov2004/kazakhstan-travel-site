USTYURT NATURE RESERVE

A unique natural reserve with Ustyurt landscapes

The Ustyurt State Nature Reserve is located in western Kazakhstan and is one of the largest protected natural areas in the country. Its landscapes include deserts, plateaus, chalk cliffs, and rare landforms found only in this region.

  • Area: over 223,000 hectares
  • Type: desert ecoregion
  • Wildlife: saiga antelopes, caracals, desert foxes
  • Access: restricted, permits required

What makes it unique?

  • 🦅 Habitat of rare and endangered species
  • 🏜 Stunning views of the Ustyurt Plateau
  • 📷 Perfect for photography and scientific expeditions

How to get there?

📍 Mangystau Region, near the border with Uzbekistan
🚗 Usually accessed via off-road vehicles with guides

Important to know

⛺ Not equipped for mass tourism
❗ Visits are only allowed with representatives of the reserve

The Ustyurt Reserve is a stunning destination for those seeking wilderness, silence, and unspoiled nature.
